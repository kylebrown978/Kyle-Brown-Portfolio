Autonomous Drone (MAV)

Role: Controls & electronics, CAD, propulsion integration

Led the flight-control stack and physical integration for a micro air vehicle. In CAD, I designed the airframe and avionics bay, created mounts for LiDAR and IMU sensors, and laid out a serviceable harness. For propulsion I ran motor/prop/ESC trades against endurance and thrust-to-weight targets, then integrated the power distribution and tuned performance on the bench and in flight.

  • CAD (SolidWorks)
  • Propulsion sizing
  • LiDAR/IMU
  • C++/MATLAB
  • Teensy 4.0

Key contributions

  • Controls: PD outer-loop with waypoint planner; telemetry + MATLAB tools for tuning.
  • Integration: Carbon-fiber/balsa frame, sensor mounts, custom PDB; labeled, serviceable wiring.
  • Propulsion: Motor/prop/ESC selection; endurance & thrust optimization with safety margins.
