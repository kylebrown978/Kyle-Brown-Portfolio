About

I’m a recent Aerospace Engineering graduate from UCLA with a strong foundation in physics, mathematics, and mechanical systems—and a passion for solving complex, real-world problems. My academic experience sharpened my skills in areas like fluid dynamics, control systems, and structural analysis, and I’ve developed proficiency in industry-standard tools such as MATLAB, SolidWorks, and C++.

I’m particularly interested in aerodynamic systems, propulsion, and spacecraft design—fields where technical precision meets creative problem solving. I also enjoy simulation and visualization tools like COMSOL to understand how systems behave and interact.

Outside of engineering, I’ve built strong interpersonal and leadership skills through several years in the hospitality industry. These experiences taught me clear communication, teamwork under pressure, and delivering consistent results—all of which I carry into technical challenges.
